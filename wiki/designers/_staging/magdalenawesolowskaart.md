  Biography


  Magdalena Wesołowska (1981) Bytom/Poland


  She studied painting at the Institute of Art in Cieszyn, a branch of the
  University of Silesia in Katowice, in the studio of Prof. Tadeusz Rus. She
  graduated in 2007. She studied drawing in the studio of Anna Kowalczyk-Klus.
  She received the 2025 Scholarship of the Marshal of the Silesian Voivodeship
  in the field of culture and she 2023 Artistic Scholarship of the City of Bytom
  from the president of Bytom. She is a member of the Association of Visual
  Artists in Katowice.




  The artist creates using the following techniques: oil and watercolor.




  The artist creates in the cycles: Identity Written in the Body, Body, Water,
  Landscape of Silesia, When Night Comes We See Differently.
