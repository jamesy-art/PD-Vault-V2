  exceptional quality eco friendly and sustainable process where re-active dyes
  are steamed deeply into the fabric fibres, for colour intensity, superb crisp
  detail, colour longevity, beautiful handle and high rub fastness. The finished
  pre-shrunk fabric has been washed in cold and then hot water and is therefor
  safe to touch and is perfectly suitable for domestic textiles, being free of

  be washed and washed without loss of print. We choose linens for their

  beauty of our printed textiles. Please ask us for a free tea towel to be
  posted to you- the proof is in the pudding! PLEASE NOTE We use natural linens
  and print our pattens on the fabric using natural, non toxic dyes, that are
  steamed into the fabric. Natural dyes will fade when exposed to harsh direct
  UV Rays. When using our linens for curtains,it is essential to please use a
  fabric backing, and also a UV film on the windows, to avoid the harsh UV rays
  from fading the print. When ordering please choose the correct fabric for
  purpose We offer a large range of fabric choices such as Libeco 445 for heavy
