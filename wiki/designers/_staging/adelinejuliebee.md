## Overview

Adeline-Julie Bee (Baes) is a Belgian watercolor artist and surface pattern designer based in the Ardennes region. Her practice combines original watercolor painting with surface pattern design for licensing, alongside a teaching practice offering watercolor courses. She maintains an active presence on Spoonflower and positions her work as licensing-ready across home decor, linen, and wallpaper.

## Style and Aesthetic

Nature-led work inspired by the flora and landscape of the Belgian Ardennes. Selected Instagram images suggest floral and botanical compositions with a soft, expressive watercolor quality. Palettes draw on seasonal natural tones. The hand-crafted, artisanal character is well-suited to home decor and stationery licensing contexts.

## Techniques and Tools

Watercolor is the primary medium. Uniquely, she creates her own natural inks and pigments from foraged Ardennes ingredients. These original watercolor works are then translated into surface design patterns and repeat constructions for licensing applications.

## Markets and Clients

Primary output targets home decor (interiors, linens, wallpaper) and stationery licensing. Available on Spoonflower. Selected placement image suggests wallpaper and home textile applications. No named corporate licensing clients documented.

## Career Path

Trained as a watercolor artist. Built a dual practice as a surface pattern designer and creative educator, teaching watercolor techniques. Developed a distinctive craft-led process using natural, foraged pigments from the Belgian Ardennes. Identified as "licensing ready" on Instagram.

## Pattern Focus

Floral, botanical, and seasonal nature motifs are the core subject matter. Patterns derive from original watercolor paintings. Selected Instagram patterns confirm botanical and floral all-over repeat work with a hand-painted quality.
