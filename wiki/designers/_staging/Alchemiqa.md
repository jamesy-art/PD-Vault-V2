## Overview

Solange Yepez is a Toronto-based surface pattern and textile designer operating under the studio name Alchemiqa. A graduate of Ryerson University's Fashion Design program, she currently serves as Lead Textile Designer and Product Development Manager at [[Andrea Iyamah]] (womenswear brand). Alchemiqa is her freelance practice, available for independent commissions.

## Style and Aesthetic

Alchemiqa positions itself as surface design "for the bold and unconventional," with a moody, fashion-forward aesthetic. Selected Instagram patterns suggest botanical and decorative compositions with rich, confident colour choices. Work spans fashion and home decor applications.

## Techniques and Tools

Combines hand-painted and digital techniques. Skills include illustration, painting, pattern design, and repeat construction. The dual approach covers both hand-crafted originals and production-ready digital patterns.

## Markets and Clients

Primary market is womenswear, with a secondary focus on home decor. Documented clients include [[Knixwear]], Gluckstein Home, and JQLYN & Co. The Anna Sui × Knix collaboration appears in her website project listing. Her most sustained employer relationship is with Andrea Iyamah, where she serves as lead textile designer.

## Portfolio and Presence

- Website: https://Alchemiqa.com

## Career Path

Graduated from Ryerson University with a Bachelor's Degree in Fashion Design. Discovered pattern design while working as a Junior Designer at Andrea Iyamah. Subsequently completed a short-term credit in Surface Design at the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD). Now holds the position of Lead Textile Designer and Product Development Manager at Andrea Iyamah while maintaining Alchemiqa as a parallel freelance studio.

## Pattern Focus

Selected Instagram patterns suggest botanical and decorative surface work. Work is positioned for both fashion (womenswear) and interior applications. Strong colour sensibility and a signature moody aesthetic differentiate the studio output.
