## Overview

Alisha de Souza is a print and graphic designer based in the United States. Her practice spans textiles, graphics, apparel design, fine art, and wedding product design. She produces hand-painted and digital illustrations adapted into repeat patterns, primarily for children's apparel and household products. Confirmed clients include Tea Collection and Coyuchi.

## Style and Aesthetic

de Souza's aesthetic moves between naive folk art and delicate watercolour painting, primarily working in ink. Her cultural background — growing up between India and the United States — informs a design sensibility rooted in diverse folk traditions. Selected Instagram images suggest illustrative pattern work with a painterly, handcrafted quality and culturally informed motifs.

## Techniques and Tools

Primarily works in ink; also watercolour and digital. Produces hand-painted illustrations adapted into repeat patterns for product development. Skills include illustration, painting, colour theory, repeat construction, and client collaboration.
