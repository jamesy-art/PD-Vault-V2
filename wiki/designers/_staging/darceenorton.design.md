website_meta_description: >-
  Darcee Norton is an illustrator and surface pattern designer who enriches her
  work with engaging, compelling, and often unexpected narratives. Working
  primarily with graphite and watercolour, she creates intricate hand drawn
  illustrations that invite closer inspection and uncover unexpected narratives.
  Her project concepts are often influenced by her fascination with historical
  contexts, allowing her imagination to run free and develop narratives that
  embrace individual expression and a sense of place. Character development and
  strong storytelling throughout her design work aim to create compelling
  stories that act as conversation starters, welcoming interactivity and
  encouraging emotional connection. As an interior focused designer, Darcee
  specialises in wallpaper and home furnishings that reflect individuality,
  appealing to a market who share a love of the weird and wonderful. Her designs
  aim to curate spaces that feel both personal and inviting, places where people
  can relax and dream of wonderful, alternative worlds.
