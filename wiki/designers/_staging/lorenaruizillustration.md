  I’m an illustrator and graphic designer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. For
  15 years, I worked in the audiovisual field, focusing on the design and
  animation of visual identities and branding for television networks. I took
  part in projects for Fox International Channels, MTV, National Geographic
  Channels, Nickelodeon, and Dmax, among others. I taught at the Faculty of
  Architecture, Design and Urbanism at the University of Buenos Aires, where I
  lectured in Typography Longinotti and Illustration Roldán. I also collaborated
  with the FADU Illustration Postgraduate Program. Since 2015, I’ve focused my
  work on illustration, creating patterns for clothing and editorial
  illustrations. I’ve collaborated with media and brands such as Jacobin
  Magazine, National Geographic, Disney, and Marvel. I currently work as an
  illustrator for videos, textile prints, and editorial projects. I’m the author
  and illustrator of the book A viajar, semillas! (Let’s Travel, Seeds!, Periplo
  Ediciones), which was selected by the New York Public Library as one of the
  Best Spanish-Language Children’s Books of 2022. In 2026, I illustrated and
  wrote “I Am Water,” published by the same publisher. Passionate about nature
  and ecology, I find my greatest inspiration in flowers, trees, and animals. To
  create my images, I cut paper shapes and hand-drawn textures, which I then
  combine digitally. I love vibrant, saturated color palettes, and I’m always
  exploring new techniques — among my favorites are monotype and risograph
  printing. More recently, I’ve incorporated watercolor and ink, moving
  naturally between handmade and digital work. CONTACT MEE MAIL
