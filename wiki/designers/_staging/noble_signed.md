  Be-You-Tiful”, a wordplay literally meaning ‘you are beautiful’ when you

  are hand painting, dyeing and weaving. The painted fabric, which is made of
  over 10,000 hand painted brush strokes, both perfect and imperfect brush
  strokes coming together to form the beauty of the fabric. This explains both
  strength and weaknesses ( characteristics ) of a person, yet when put
  together, brings the beauty in them. The union reveals clarity, imperfections,
  and uncertainties of the human race. Cohesion of selected techniques and
  materials well manipulated, reveals the truth and pricks the conscience
