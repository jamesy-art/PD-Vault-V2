   Hello! 

  I’m Pooja and I am a passionate illustrator with a love for all things floral
  and botanical. My artistic journey is a delightful blend of traditional and
  digital mediums, with a focus on creating whimsical and joyful illustrations.


  As an artist, I find inspiration in the beauty of nature, particularly in the
  delicate petals of flowers and the intricate details of botanical wonders.
  Most of my artwork is brought to life through digital means, allowing me to
  explore a vast palette of colors and bring out the magical essence in my
  illustrations.


  One of my favorite pastimes is taking leisurely walks in the park, where I
  find joy in foraging for wildflowers. These nature excursions serve as both a
  source of relaxation and a treasure trove of inspiration. The wildflowers I
  collect during these walks become the muses for my creations, adding an
  authentic and organic touch to my illustrations.


  My artistic style is characterized by its whimsy and playfulness, each piece
  weaving a unique narrative. I believe that art should not only be visually
  appealing but also convey emotions and stories that resonate with the viewer.
  Through my illustrations, I aim to transport you to a world filled with
  vibrant hues, blooming florals, and the magic of storytelling.


  In addition to creating personal artworks, I am thrilled to offer
  commission-based services. If you're in search of beautifully designed wedding

  botanical magic.
