## Overview

Althea Studio is a small design studio currently based in Barcelona, operating between Buenos Aires, Mexico, and Paris. Founded by two practitioners: Constanza Macris (fashion designer and brand consultant) and Juana Ferreira (visual artist, illustrator, and designer). The studio delivers print design, garment development, and brand strategy to fashion clients. Primary documented client is Argentine fashion brand Mariacher, with confirmed seasonal supply across more than ten consecutive collections.

## Style and Aesthetic

Selected Instagram images suggest a print practice combining hand-drawn and collage-influenced mark-making with considered graphic composition. Work reads as culturally specific and narrative-driven rather than formula-trend. All five selected images are pattern-category, indicating print design is the studio's primary visual output.

## Techniques and Tools

Hand-drawn and digital print development. Delivers placement prints and all-over prints, colorways, and production-ready files. Jacquard knit design and development documented (Mariacher ss24, hs24; Carolina K aw25). Embroidery design executed across multiple Mariacher seasons, with embroidery production references in India indicating international supply chain coordination. Tech pack production and garment development included in the service range.

## Markets and Clients

[[Fashion]] (primary). Confirmed client: Mariacher (Argentina) — print and garment design across hs24, aw24, ps25, hs23, aw22, aw23, resort24, ps24, resort25, ss24, ss23, and resort25 seasons. Named prints include ceibo, atacalco, madariaga, alagon, varela, amazona, bogado, casares, carranza, and bernal. Confirmed client: Carolina K — knit design and development, aw25.

## Career Path

Studio founded by two designers with complementary backgrounds: Macris with fashion design, creative direction, and garment development experience (from Buenos Aires, based in Paris); Ferreira with costume and set design training and a cross-disciplinary visual arts practice. Currently operating from Barcelona with project work across Latin America and Europe.

## Pattern Focus

Print design for fashion collections, combining hand-drawn mark-making with digital production. Selected images suggest abstract and figurative print compositions — placement and all-over prints for seasonal fashion deployment. The depth of the Mariacher seasonal record confirms a sustained commercial print practice rather than one-off commissions.
