  focus on drawing, painting and mixing colour from observation. After

  designer I have a background in originating artwork, colour sampling, liaising

  translated to screen for printing. The screen-printing technique allows for
  further expression and experimentation using different combinations of colour,
  pattern and texture. The hand-printed textiles and everything connected with

  screens and a purpose built print table. Water based pigment dyes are
