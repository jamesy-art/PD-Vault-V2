## Overview

Alexandra Galibert is an illustrator and surface pattern designer originally from France, now based in Cheshire, UK. She is represented by [[A Fresh Bunch]] licensing agency and is available for licensing, collaborations, and commissions. Her work is inspired by nature and animals, with a focus on joyful, whimsical storytelling through pattern and illustration.

## Style and Aesthetic

Galibert's aesthetic is characterised by joyful, everyday storytelling — nature, animals, garden motifs, and quiet domestic subjects rendered in a cute, accessible illustrative style. Selected Instagram images suggest decorative repeat pattern work with warm palettes and a handcrafted, layered quality. Her process (sketch → colour → texture → final) indicates a considered, multi-stage approach to each piece.

## Techniques and Tools

Illustrative pattern design built from hand-drawn sketches with layered colour and texture. Skills listed include illustration, pattern design, communication, licensing basics, and client collaboration. Process documentation on her website demonstrates a structured approach from initial sketch to finished pattern.
