website_about_clean: >-
  Hi! I'm Kaisa. My journey into the world of patterns and illustrations began
  in 2014 while I was on maternity leave. Since I was a child, I have enjoyed
  drawing and painting. Some of my best childhood memories are the times I spent
  painting with my father. At some point, on the threshold of my teenage years,
  however, making pictures took a back seat in my life for many years. Over the
  years, I also developed quite a fear of white paper. The feeling that I should
  know how to do it right away, create "masterpieces" and be "ready", whatever
  that means... Somewhere in the back of my mind, however, I always had a dream
  of starting the hobby again. While I was on maternity leave, I also felt a
  longing for some kind of personal time. With a baby, everything was a whole
  new learning experience. The thought came to my mind that maybe you don't have
  to know everything right away when it comes to taking pictures, but you could
  give yourself permission to be a beginner and take the time to learn things.
  From that moment on, I immersed myself in the world of studying illustrations
  and patterns. Nowadays, we are lucky to have numerous online tutorials on the
  internet that allow you to study almost anything independently. A learning
  path began, during which you must have watched hundreds of hours of various
  instructional videos on painting, illustration, pattern design and using
  Photoshop. ​ As a result of these years of experimentation, I have developed
  my own style of doing things. A style that is cheerful and colorful. These
  pages are my electronic portfolio of my patterns and illustrations. Welcome to
  my colorful world!
