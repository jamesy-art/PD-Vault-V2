## Overview

Alicia Hayes is a freelance surface pattern designer and artist based in Draper, Utah, USA, with over 10 years of professional practice. She works in watercolour, mixed media, and digital formats, creating art for products, publications, and licensing. Original paintings have been sold worldwide; her mixed media work has been featured in Stampington & Company. A full licensing portfolio is available to trade buyers on request.

## Style and Aesthetic

Hayes's work is consistently described as whimsical and soulful — featuring strong women, cute animals, colourful flowers, and food. The aesthetic is warm and narrative-driven, typically beginning as a standalone illustration intended for greeting cards or wall art, which is then extended into coordinating pattern sets. Selected Instagram images suggest vibrant illustrative pattern work with a hand-painted character.

## Techniques and Tools

Watercolour, mixed media, and digital. Produces original paintings that are adapted into patterns and product applications. Website categories include wall art, greeting cards, patterns, custom home portraits, and pet portraits. Published multiple times in Stampington & Company.
