## Overview

Aina M Snape is a textile print designer based in Mallorca, Spain, specializing in contemporary surface design for fashion collections. With a Fine Arts background (Barcelona Fine Arts University and FD Moda – LCI Barcelona) and over ten years of experience in textile graphics, she operates as a studio offering exclusive, commission-based print work delivered in repeat.

## Style and Aesthetic

Specializes in florals, placement prints, and abstract artwork. Practice balances artistic expression with commercial awareness — producing visually distinct prints that remain wearable and market-relevant. Selected Instagram patterns suggest clean, contemporary compositions suitable for fashion across multiple demographics.

## Techniques and Tools

Combines hand drawing and hand painting with digital finishing. All prints are delivered in repeat. Range covers both placement prints and all-over repeat construction.

## Markets and Clients

Contemporary fashion is the primary market — womenswear, menswear, and childrenswear. Work is developed for fashion brands and suppliers. Commissions welcome. No named clients documented in available sources.

## Career Path

Studied Fine Arts at the Barcelona Fine Arts University and textile design at FD Moda – LCI Barcelona. Has worked in textile fashion for over ten years, building a studio practice in exclusive print development. Based in Mallorca.

## Pattern Focus

Core specialisms are florals, tropical motifs, placement prints, and abstract artwork. Selected Instagram patterns confirm commercially viable all-over and placement print compositions for contemporary fashion.
