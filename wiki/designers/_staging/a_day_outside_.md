## Overview

Sara Pizzinelli is an Italian graphic designer and illustrator based in Italy. She works across books, magazines, visual identities, animations, and digital assets for brands in the visual arts and fashion sectors. Her Instagram practice, under the handle @a_day_outside_, centres on atmospheric illustration with a lyrical, dreamlike sensibility. Selected Instagram images suggest a soft, nature-inspired illustration practice with an emerging surface pattern application.

## Style and Aesthetic

Atmospheric and introspective. Instagram bio describes "daydream landscapes and soft fancies." Selected pattern images suggest a muted, contemplative aesthetic — soft organic forms, nature-inspired compositions, and a restrained colour palette. The visual register is illustrative and quietly poetic rather than bold or commercial in orientation.

## Techniques and Tools

Trained in graphic design and illustration. Works across book design, magazine layouts, visual identity, animation, and digital assets. Illustration is used as a communicative medium across both commercial and personal work. No specific software stated.

## Markets and Clients

Visual arts, fashion, performance arts, photography, and literature are the stated client sectors. Has worked for several artists and festivals across these areas. Open to commissions. Surface pattern represents an extension of the illustration practice rather than a dedicated commercial track at present.
