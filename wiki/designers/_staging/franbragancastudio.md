  Our History Fran Bragança is a graphic designer and illustrator based in Rio
  de Janeiro, Brazil, since 2003. A graduate in product design from PUC-RJ, she
  has worked for brands such as Richards and Francesca Romana Diana. In 2019,
  she returned to her roots, exploring hand-painting in watercolor and gouache
  to create patterns. Combining these two skills, she has been creating patterns
  inspired by the interior of Rio de Janeiro. Her love for flowers, fruits,
  animals, and traditional elements can be seen regularly in her work. Her three
  children are also a great inspiration. Recently, she completed another course
  in botanical drawing and watercolor at the National School of Tropical Botany
  in Rio de Janeiro, further refining her paintings.
