## Overview

Alex Foxley is a freelance illustrator based in Barcelona, Spain, specialising in mascot design, illustrated branding, patterns, and apparel artwork for character-driven brands. He runs Studio Foxley, a branding and design studio. His pattern work explores abstract forms with limited palettes, created for apparel and textile applications.

## Style and Aesthetic

Foxley's visual language is bold, colourful, and character-driven. His Instagram bio positions his work as "illustrations, patterns & characters" — and website keywords ("colourful, pop art") confirm a graphic, high-energy sensibility. Selected Instagram pattern images suggest work with abstract, geometric, and illustrative elements: strong colour, clear form, and a pop-art approach applied to repeat design.

## Techniques and Tools

Digital illustration. Works across mascot design, branding illustration, pattern design, and apparel graphics. Skills listed include illustration, graphic design, colour theory, market adaptation, and client collaboration.
