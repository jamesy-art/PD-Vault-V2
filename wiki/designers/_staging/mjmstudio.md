  Jacqueline Maldonado is a surface pattern designer and watercolor artist.


  As a New York native, currently residing in Long Island, she spent most of her

  watercolor. 


  Her abstracts in watercolor can be understood as emotional landscapes
  expressed as color and movement, communicating the essence of an experience;
  music, a memory, loss, joy, uncertainty, defeat, triumph, love and longing.
  She is drawn to pattern, finding inspiration in the repetition of forms and
  forces of nature and the world around her. 


  Jacqueline had the honor of creating art for the Starbucks Spring  2023 ad
  campaign by The Integer Group. Her print, originally titled, “Beta” was
  featured as the background art for online and print advertising as well as the
  surface pattern design for point of purchase displays and mobile events for
  the worldwide campaign.


  Jacqueline’s work is featured as home decor products on sites like

  Beyond, Macy’s, Amazon, Joss & Main, One King's Lane, PacSun, Zulily, Wayfair
  and many others. 


  One highlight from her career that Jacqueline is proud of is her partnership
  with Popsockets, contributing to  a charity campaign benefiting Days For
  Girls, Int’l.
