manual_about_raw: >-

  A little about me...


  After many years as commercial graphic designer I have decided to follow my
  passion for creating patterns for decor, both wallpaper and fabric.

  I am passionate about interior spaces and how colour and pattern can transform
  and uplift a room.

  My work often begins with hand-drawn motifs, inspired by nature, vintage
  design and travel, before evolving into prints for wallpaper and fabric.

  It is always my goal to create designs that bring joy, personality, and a
  sense of story into a space.

  I love to create patterns that celebrate the natural world and the playfulness
  of design. Whether sketching plants, carving linocuts, or working digitally,
  I’m always exploring ways to translate texture, movement, and mood into
  designs for wallpaper and textiles.

  Most of what you see here is available on Spoonflower in multiple colour ways
  ~ click on SHOP to go straight there.

  Enjoy!!

## Overview

Tracey Johnson is an Australian surface pattern designer based in Nelson Bay, NSW, trading as A Trace of Design. A former commercial graphic designer, she transitioned into pattern design driven by a passion for interiors, colour, and pattern. Her work is primarily distributed through [[Spoonflower]], where her shop carries over 800 designs across fabric and wallpaper, with custom colour and scale adaptations available.

## Style and Aesthetic

Selected Instagram patterns suggest nature-inspired all-over compositions with a hand-drawn quality. Spoonflower design names indicate an eclectic, maximalist-leaning range: "Groovy 70s Waves," "Twisted Tiger Tango," "Boho Seahorse Love," "Vibrant Hothouse Floral," "Night Time Monkey Mischief." The overall aesthetic spans mid-century retro, tropical, botanical, boho, and vintage florals — warm, layered, and interior-friendly.

## Techniques and Tools

Core process begins with hand-drawn motifs, inspired by nature, vintage design, and travel. Also explores linocut carving. Work is produced digitally for Spoonflower, with designs available in multiple colorways. Custom Pantone and scale adaptations available on request.

## Markets and Clients

Almost entirely home decor — wallpaper and fabric. Designs sold on Spoonflower as fabric and wallpaper, with 828+ listings. Custom colorway and scale services available via direct inquiry. No named licensing clients documented.
