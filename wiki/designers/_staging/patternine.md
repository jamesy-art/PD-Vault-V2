  boundaries of digital design. One language. Endless surface application.
  Patternine designs and licenses original surface and graphic patterns for use
  across architecture, materials, and products. Each design is scalable,
  adaptable, and built to travel, from personal objects and intimate interiors
  to large scale installations. Our work adorns architectural walls and
  ceilings, hard surfaces, textiles, furniture, moving objects, and product
  collections. • Architectural walls and ceilings • Hard surface and composite
  materials • Textiles and soft goods • Furniture, fixtures and accessories •
  Moving objects, and transportation. • Home and lifestyle products. Design is a
  conversation between logic and wonder. We study waves folding onto shore, wind
  shaping sand, light tracing stone. Those movements become systems. Those
  systems become form. The result is work that is structured, yet free, precise,
  and rhythmic, while maintaining a signature of human authorship. Craig Woehrle
  started Patternine to explore how the language of nature and it's processes
  can inform/become design. Trained in architecture and landscape architecture,
  his interest was bent toward parametric systems; drawing inspiration from
  diverse areas of inquiry; from art to mathematics, he sought tools to
  translate encounters with the world, into sculpted patterns. Retrospecta Urban
