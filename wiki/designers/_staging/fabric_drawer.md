  surface designer and maker living in Melbourne, Australia, who has a love of
  creating patterns. Whilst working as a graphic designer for many years and
  yearning to create something more tactile, I enrolled into a screen-printing
  course, which led to start a textiles course and discover the world of
  repeating patterns. I started Fabric Drawer in 2012 after travelling and then
  living in London, spending many hours wondering how to follow my creative
  passions. Things started very small and organically, by being involved in a
  few small craft markets selling products I’d designed and made. Not deterred
  by the minimal sales, I continued to create my patterns and products and have
  been part of many design markets in Melbourne. As well as making my own range
  of products, I also worked with an art agent in America, licensing my artwork
  on a range gift/party ware and greeting cards. Since flying solo, I’ve
  collaborated with a range of local and international companies. Outside of
  Fabric Drawer, I’m a mum to two daughters, enjoy running and still work as a
