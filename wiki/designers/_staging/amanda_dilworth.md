## Overview

UK-based watercolour artist and illustrator specialising in food illustration, nature subjects, and surface pattern. Works across commissions, licensing, and publishing. A documented client list includes packaging work for Deliciously Ella, a heavily illustrated nature book for Weidenfeld & Nicolson at Orion Publishing (80+ interior illustrations), and product and homeware commissions for multiple design agencies. Open to commissions.

## Style and Aesthetic

Hand-rendered watercolour with a focus on richly detailed, nature-informed imagery. Selected patterns suggest botanical, food, and organic motifs executed with a warm, painterly approach. The medium and subject matter position her work within premium illustrated print territory — detailed enough for editorial use, structured enough for surface pattern application in home and stationery contexts.

## Techniques and Tools

Watercolour (primary medium), hand-rendered with digital production output. Experienced in licence agreements and digital artwork delivery per client testimonials. Manages direct client relationships independently across packaging, publishing, and homeware projects.
