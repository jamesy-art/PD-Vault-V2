  BIOGRAPHY

  The founder's story


  Céline Theis is a textile designer, curious and passionate about patterns and
  colors. Born in Belgium, she demonstrated a creative streak and a strong
  attraction to art from an early age. As a teenager, she moved to Antibes,
  France, with her family. She continued her studies in Marseille, where she
  developed a passion for photography and illustration. Her desire to deepen her
  artistic knowledge led her to Milan, where she graduated in graphic design
  from the European Institute of Design (IED). It was there that she began her
  career in the print industry, and after a few years, she decided to explore
  the fashion industry. She worked closely with Carla Sozzani at 10 Corso Como
  and was fascinated by fabrics and the way designs come to life when worn. This
  discovery led her to expand her professional experience with new experiences
  in the luxury sector, working as a textile designer for apparel and
  accessories for the fashion house Etro. 


  In 2022, she decided to make her dream come true and founded the silk
  accessories brand "Mon Lien Atelier"  to give free rein to her personal
  imagination. 




  THE IDEA

  The brand


  Mon Lien , or "my bond," aims to highlight the importance of relationships
  with the special people in our lives through an object that is, first and
  foremost, a vehicle of emotion. It represents the physical connection between
  the accessory and our body when we tie it like a precious seal. It expresses
  the common thread that connects these great passions: fashion, patterns, and
  colors.


  Atelier symbolizes a place of creativity, a small space where art is
  conceived. It emphasizes the craftsmanship of the product, crafted with care
  and in limited quantities.


  Mon Lien Atelier is a world of rich, detailed graphics designed in
  sophisticated structures. The creative process begins with a freehand sketch,
  then continues with the digital composition of the elements, and concludes
  with the search for the right color tone to achieve the perfect harmony. Each
  mini-collection is characterized by a certain continuity, where the same
  graphic elements are structured differently for a perfect match between the
  different formats of clutches, scarves, stoles, and sashes. They are so
  differently similar.

   THE PRODUCT
  The message


  "To wrap people in a colorful, emotionally charged silk embrace, to accompany
  them wherever they choose to go."


  The Color


  Teal , a sophisticated blend of blue and green with a hint of gray. A bold
  shade somewhere between turquoise and petrol. Since its discovery in the late
  1950s, it has been a symbol of elegance and exclusivity.




  THE PRODUCTION

  Strictly "Made in Italy"


  Each product is printed and hemmed in Como , home to the finest luxury fashion
  manufacturers, an Italian excellence recognized worldwide for its expertise
  and passion for crafting the highest quality products.

   The Fabric

  All items are made of 100% silk twill . This fascinating natural fiber, known
  for its elegance and excellent color rendition, is used to create high-quality
  fabrics. Lightweight, hypoallergenic, and soft to the touch, it is highly
