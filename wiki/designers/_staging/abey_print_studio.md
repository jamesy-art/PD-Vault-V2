manual_about_raw: >-
  Hellooo! 


  Thanks for checking me out! 


  I’m Nisha Abey, an artist and textile print designer based on the East Coast
  of Australia - usually in Brisbane or Byron Bay.  I specialise in hand painted
  and hand drawn artwork, using acrylic, watercolour and ink to create original
  art for surfaces and patterns. Working mainly with Fashion and Homewares
  brands, I love personally talking about every individual project and delving
  into it to ensure even brushstroke reflects the idea. 


  My style is expressive, painterly and I like to paint with emotion in a
  meditative state. To me, words evoke emotion and understanding the story
  behind a print, moodboard or idea allow me to create and paint authentic, one
  of a kind artwork for individuals and brands alike.


  With 15 years of experience in Fashion and Homewares across Design and
  Production in Europe and Australia, I work collaboratively with emerging and
  established brands and companies with all their print needs. 


  A bit about me; 


  Background - I am an English girl with a very multi cultural background. My
  parents from Trinidad and Sri Lanka and I decided to move to Australia in
  2013.


  Fave Motif - I am a sucker for botanicals, and can’t go past. bunch of flowers
  without taking a million photos for future print ideas.


  Dream Holiday -  I would love to visit Italy, New Zealand and Japan sometime
  soon


  Fave Quote - “I found I could say things with colour and shapes that I
  couldn’t say any other way - things I had no words for.” - Georgia O’Keeffe


  Wildest Dream - Being a finalist (and winning) the Archibald prize!


  Let’s chat all things creative, you project or just to connect >>> Who is By
  Abey 

  Abey Print Studio is an independent print studio run by Artist and Designer
  Nisha Abey based between Brisbane and Byron Bay, Australia specialising in
  surface and textile pattern design. We paint and draw one of a kind prints and
  exclusive artworks for Fashion, Interior brands and personal commissions. 


  With over 15 years of experience in Fashion and Homewares across Design and
  Production in Europe and Australia, we support and elevate new brands as well
  as provide established companies with any print and ranging needs. 


  Every print has a story, and we love to talk through any ideas to really
  understand your customer, the design you are looking for and any finer
  details, before hand painting, drawing or working digitally to create a page
  of motifs and seamless repeat artwork.


  We love a collaborative and creative process on every print, from the initial
  design consultation to finalising a repeat design you will love. 


  Style of Artwork 

  Coming from a Print Agency background means we are a multi disciplinary in all
  design and artwork and can paint and draw most styles to fit most brands.


  Take your pick form the huge range of print categories for your collection -
  botanical, animal, paisley, chintz, scarf prints, patchwork, boho border,
  non-prints, conversational and novelty to name just a view. 


  Combining old traditional techniques, painting styles and heritage to make
  them modern and expressive - we are inspired by history, nature and always on
  top of trends happening now. 


  Drawing inspiration from my adoration with style, colour, interiors and
  details as well as our own English - West Indian heritage to refine concepts
  into a design. From ethnic paisleys and intricate chintz to vintage Liberty
  ditsy’s and watercolour botanicals - our ideas are inspired from everything
  and anything but with beauty and femininity are at the core of our design.


  Pricing

  We are pretty competitive with pricing within the industry standard. 


  Price depending on the intricacy of the print, in particular a screen
  printable vector artwork compared to a watercolour hand painted botanical. 


  Please contact us to get an accurate quote for the design you need.


  Timeframe

  We usually get booked up quite quickly, so allow between 2 weeks to book in a
  design slot. 


  Once your job is booked in we can often deliver a print within a week
  depending on the intricacy of the motifs, repeat and as long as the brief and
  print direction is clear. 


  Saying this, we can and do help brands with urgent and a quick turnaround for
  an additional cost - please just email and advise the urgency and we can try
  to help you out. 


  Modifications and Changes 

  Every custom print job allows 2 print changes within the cost of the print
  which can be advised in email or over the phone. 


  Any additional changes are an additional cost.


  We recommend being as clear with the project and print direct at the initial
  free design consultation and the follow up meetings. 


  We can supply a brief sheet to get you started with the information we need to
  know before we start a brief.


  Print Showings

  I do a quarterly print showing with the Seasonal Collection. Sign up to the
  Newsletter to find out when the new Collection will be available or email to
  book to find out more and book this on first hand. 


  We offer both face-to-face print showings in Australia and London and virtual
  print showings all year round.


  Can I visit your Studio?

  We do not have a specific office space but we are happy to meet you at a cafe
  in Brisbane or organise a face-to-face call. Happy to discuss the best option
  for you.  


  Contact us if you have any further question - we are happy to chat! 


  Email info@abeyprintstudio.com

## Overview

Nisha Abey is an Australian textile print designer and artist running Abey Print Studio, an independent practice based between Brisbane and Byron Bay. With over 15 years of experience in design, production, and fabric roles across London and Australia, she specializes in hand-painted and hand-drawn artwork for fashion and homeware brands. The studio offers bespoke commissions, collaborative print development, and quarterly seasonal print showings for trade buyers.

## Style and Aesthetic

Abey's visual signature is expressive, painterly, and feminine. Selected portfolio images show a range spanning lush botanical and floral arrangements to more structured all-over repeat constructions — all executed with visible brushwork and warm, saturated colour. Influences include vintage design, traditional art techniques, heritage motifs, and her multicultural English-Caribbean-Sri Lankan background. The consistent thread is handcrafted warmth: even digitally finished work retains an organic, hand-originated quality.

## Techniques and Tools

Specializes in watercolour and acrylic hand painting, pencil and ink drawing, and digital/Procreate production. Medium selection is determined by brief requirements. Works with multiple colourways and builds seamless repeat constructions.

## Markets and Clients

Fashion (womenswear) and homewares are the primary sectors. Documented client: OLI & KUBO (complex hand-drawn and painted prints, multiple projects). Studio also serves personal commission clients and supports in-house design teams. Quarterly print showings available to trade buyers in Australia and London, with virtual options year-round.

## Career Path

Originally from London, with Sri Lankan, Trinidadian, and English heritage. Spent approximately 19 years in design, production, and fabric roles across London and Australia. Founded Abey Print Studio to centre her practice on pattern, colour, trend, and art. Relocated to Byron Bay and Brisbane, Australia.

## Pattern Focus

Selected portfolio images confirm a botanical and floral-led practice with strong painterly execution — arrangements appear as loose, expressive all-over repeat constructions rather than tight engineered layouts. Additional portfolio selections show coordinating pattern ranges, suggesting a multi-piece collection approach. Print categories extend to chintz, paisley, ethnic motifs, scarf prints, patchwork, and conversationals. Heritage and vintage references are evident in the decorative density and palette warmth across the portfolio set.
