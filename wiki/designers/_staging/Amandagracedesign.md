## Overview

US-based surface pattern designer, educator, and licensing artist operating under the Amanda Grace Design brand. Instagram-verified with 44k followers and an established presence as an educator in the English-language SPD community. Founded PatternPAL — a free seamless pattern tester reported to have processed nearly 100,000 patterns — and teaches courses, coaching, and group programmes alongside an active licensing practice. Lists 25 years in tech and integrates AI-for-business tools into her teaching model.

## Style and Aesthetic

Procreate-native practice with selected patterns suggesting a commercially accessible motif range — floral, seasonal, and decorative subjects suited to mid-market licensing. Work is positioned for broad licensability rather than a distinctive signature aesthetic.

## Techniques and Tools

[[Procreate]] (primary tool, explicitly listed). Digital workflow with emphasis on seamless repeat construction. PatternPAL is her own free online pattern testing tool.

## Markets and Clients

[[Licensing]] (designs on products worldwide, per own description). Education and coaching services: courses, 1:1 coaching, and Creative Systems Lab group programme. Specific licensees not named in available evidence.

Came to surface pattern design in her 40s following a 25-year technology career. Self-described late bloomer who now operates a full education and licensing business. Courses, 1:1 coaching, and a newsletter are core to the platform.

## Pattern Focus

Seamless repeat patterns produced in [[Procreate]]. Selected patterns suggest a broad, licensable aesthetic covering floral and decorative motifs. Work positioned for multi-market licensing across [[Stationery]], home, and gift categories.
