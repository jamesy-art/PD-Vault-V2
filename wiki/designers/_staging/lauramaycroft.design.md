  Pattern Designer & Illustrator with a versatile print hand-writing that ranges
  from conversational & illustrative to bold & graphic. Laura is constantly
  researching; from looking out for interesting stories to tell with her prints
  to understanding the context in which they will be used. Laura’s work is often
  concerned with place and how it affects wellbeing; from unexpected details to
  her confident use of colour. Her creative practice draws on her fascination
  with print-making & creating repeat patterns and these processes inform her
  work, whether hand- or digitally-printed. She focuses on innovative approaches
  to drawing for risograph & screen-printing; illustrating in layers with
  technical application in mind. Laura has designed for a range of brands - with
  her prints appearing on giftwrap, homeware, needlepoint kits and more - and
  she takes satisfaction in seeing a concept through to final product. Laura
