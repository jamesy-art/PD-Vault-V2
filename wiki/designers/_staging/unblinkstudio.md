  territory and product category covered by the license. You do not need to
  credit me as the designer, but it&rsquo;s always appreciated! It depends on
  the complexity and popularity of the design you wish to license, on the scope
  of the license, and on other factors. Also, I can usually offer
  &ldquo;bulk&rdquo; discounts if you would like to license 5 designs or more at
  one time. I understand that every project is different, so just contact me to
  discuss your particular needs and I will do what I can to accommodate you. I
  charge a flat fee which is payable once we have signed the licensing
  agreement. This fee is NON-refundable once I send the artwork file to you. In
  some cases, I will also accept royalties based on sales, usually with a
  non-refundable advance. Therefore, I usually can&rsquo;t offer you exclusivity
  on designs already in my portfolio. But, if you would like an exclusive
  design, I can create one specifically for you! Once complete, you would own
  the copyright to the design and I would not be able to use it for any other
  purpose. What if I want to license a design, but I need it in another
  colourway or want some other changes? You cannot modify a design except with
  my prior written approval. However, I can almost always accommodate colour
  revisions and even some design revisions to suit your needs, and usually
  charge an hourly rate to complete the changes for you. I can sometimes offer
  other options, but will be considered on a case-by-case basis. &#8203;&#8203;
