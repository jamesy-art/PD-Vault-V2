I didn’t grow up dreaming of becoming a surface designer—I just never stopped loving picture books, weird animals, and color palettes found in the most random places. Before patterns and designs came into my life, I earned a degree in education sciences and spent years leading creative activities for kids in schools and museums in Rome. That phase taught me a lot—how kids think, laugh, and spot the tiniest details adults often miss. Later, I launched Pollice di Panda, my first little Italian brand, creating playful activity books and printables for kids.
