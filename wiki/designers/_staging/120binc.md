  ILLUSTRATION & DESIGN


  Design Portfolio

  Hello, my name is Robin Chakraborty. I'm a freelance illustrator and designer
  based in Mumbai. With an industry experience of 8+ years. I have worked on
  multitudes of design and illustration projects, which include art direction,
  brand identity, package design, and marketing creatives.


  Native Again Branding

  Native Again is a startup aiming to market indigenous grains and oils in
  India. They wanted to re-launch some of the healthiest food grains and oils
  that were used in India traditionally but have been lost since.

  I collaborated with them to create their brand identity, helping to showcase
  their wide range of indigenous grains, pulses and flours. Native Again
  embraces the heirloom wisdom of ancient agricultural practices to create
  sustainable food and preserve ecosystems.

  They have recently also ventured into plant bases Soaps and Cleaners.
  Expanding their all natural products.


  ByVitality Package Design

  ByVitality is a premium, plant-based supplements brand. I worked with their
  team to create a unique package design and showcase the health benefits and
  soothing effects of their products. The goal was to use pastel colors with
  interesting color combinations to stand apart from the competition in the
  supplement market.


  Fitchimp Branding & Design

  Fitchimp is a premium whey protein powder and supplement brand designed to
  support muscle growth, enhance recovery, and boost overall performance. I
  worked with their team to create an enticing logo and package design.


  Titan Watch Interface Design

  Watch Interfaces created as mockups for Titan as a pitch. The idea was to
  create several interface designs for different user base.


  Hershey's Creatives

  Key Visuals and Marketing creatives made for Hershey's Choco tubes.


  Jumboking Key Visuals

  Key visuals created for Jumbo King's new Schezwan and Peri Peri Nachos
  Burgers. The brief was to show the spiciness of the burgers through red hot
  visuals.


  Levi's Creatives

  Marketing creatives made for Levi's in collaboration with Webchutney.


  StockGro Creatives

  Social Media Infographics created for StockGro. The brief was to condense all
  the content about various celebrities and topics interestingly.


  Print ad created for StockGro showcasing their T20 match tickets promotion.


  Teji Mandi Creatives

  Performance and Marketing creatives made for Teji Mandi app.


  Jio World Drive Creatives

  Marketing creatives made for Jio World Drive in collaboration with Togglehead.


  Federal Bank Creatives

  Marketing creatives made for Federal Bank in collaboration with Webchutney.

## Overview

Robin Chakraborty is a Mumbai-based freelance illustrator and designer with 8+ years of professional experience. His practice spans character illustration, brand identity, packaging, and marketing creatives for major Indian and international clients. Selected Instagram images suggest an illustrative, character-driven visual style. Surface pattern design is one strand of a broader commercial illustration and design practice.

## Style and Aesthetic

Character-based illustration with a whimsical and playful sensibility. Selected pattern images suggest figurative and character-driven compositions — consistent with a narrative illustration approach. Warm, inviting palette with an emphasis on storytelling over formal repeat structure. Self-described as drawing "cute characters, whimsical landscapes, and interesting stories."

## Techniques and Tools

Digital illustration and design. Works across illustration, brand identity, packaging design, and marketing creatives. Also produces animation (Metanoia, animated short film, 2016) and storybook illustration. No specific software stated; digital art is the primary medium.

## Markets and Clients

Commercial illustration across FMCG, finance, fashion, and social media. Documented clients: Coca-Cola (Diwali Campaign), Hershey's (Choco Tubes), Levi's (via Webchutney), Titan Watch (UI interface mockups), Jumboking, StockGro, Teji Mandi, Federal Bank (via Webchutney), Jio World Drive (via Togglehead), Post Office Studios (social media illustration series). Brand identity and packaging: Native Again (indigenous grains brand), ByVitality (plant-based supplements), Fitchimp (protein supplements). Storybook illustration: *What's in my Dad's Belly?* (with Pooja Pole).
